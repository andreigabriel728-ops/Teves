About No Poverty (SDG 1)

SDG 1 aims to eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere by a target date and reduce the proportion of men, women and children living in poverty in all its dimensions according to national definitions.

You can use this section to add context, local targets, and links to official UN pages or national reports.
